Olympic Success & Wealth of Nations

This is a reimagination of a Gapminder visualization made famous by Hans Rosling to show the relationship between income and Olympic success. It shows GDP per capita, PPP & inflation-adjusted in USD (x), cumulative Summer Olympic medal count (y) and population (area of circle) of 194 nations over the last 123 years, colored by region.
